Effective Insurance Regulatory Development Authorities – Agent License Portal (2002)
License maintenance portal for generation of New & Renewal agent licenses by all the Insurance Companies in India. Road Transport Office (RTO) – e-RTA for Vehicle Registration Portal (2003)
E-RTA portal for both Learning & Permanent Driving Licenses for GOA – RTA which has for the first time in India - KIOSK’s introduced for issue of learning licenses.
